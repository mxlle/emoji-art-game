How to play

{{ roles }} You are one team - divided into painters and buyers
{{ themes }} Two themes are revealed at the beginning of each round
{{ demand }} Buyers ask painters for an amount of paintings (demand)
{{ paint }} Painters deliver paintings from their gallery and assign them to the themes
{{ faker }} Oh no! 4 more paintings are smuggled in by the faker
{{ buy }} Buyers try to match the paintings to the themes. But don't fall for the fakes!
{{ evaluate }} Showtime: Did you choose your teammates' paintings or has the faker deceived you?
{{ repeat }} Repeat until your team bought 6 fakes. How long can you go?
{{ jokers }} Use jokers to improve your chances and win them back by doing everything right
